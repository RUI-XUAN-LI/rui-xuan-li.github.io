About me

I am an engineer at Network and Information Security Lab (NISL) of Tsinghua University. My supervisors are Baojun Liu and Prof. Haixin Duan.

My research focuses on network security and Internet measurement, with particular interests in security of the Domain Name System (DNS) and Email.

I obtained my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees from Zhejiang Gongshang University in 2021 and 2024 respectively (advised by Prof. Jun Shao).
